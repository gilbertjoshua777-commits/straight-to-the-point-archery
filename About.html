About Us

Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen. Our state-of-the-art educational and training facility combines detailed technical video analysis with generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors.

We are an indoor training facility with 28 shooting lanes (14 ten-yard lanes and 14 twenty-yard lanes) and 2 private coaching rooms. We also have access to 26 additional twenty-yard lanes and an outdoor shooting field. We offer several types of lessons to accommodate everyone from first-time archers to professional archers.

Once set up with properly fitting equipment, each archer receives basic safety instruction and is introduced to various shooting techniques. From beginner to elite, archers of all ages and abilities learn about this exciting Olympic and international sport here.

You can sign up for group lessons, private classes, birthday or corporate events, and tournaments. We also offer homeschooling programs, youth clubs, YMCA classes, Boy and Girl Scouts programs, and other community activities.

Our instructors are Level 1 and Level 2 certified coaches through USA Archery (USAA), with experience across recreational, competitive, and bow hunting disciplines. We maintain strong connections with national and state archery organizations, ensuring high standards of safety and instruction.

Straight to the Point Archery is committed to teaching valuable life skills through the discipline of archery in a safe, positive, and supportive environment.
